Suggestions and issues for the Open Energy Dashboard

We welcome your thoughts on this project. This could be a request for a feature, a suggestion on something already in OED, or a bug/issue you have encountered. There are two ways to let us know:

  • Send us an email: to make a suggest or to report a bug or issue
  • Use our GitHub Repository to submit an issue.

You should expect to hear back from us about your input and how we plan to deal with it.
